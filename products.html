YGMI Blockchain based job Search


More than just a typical job search platform. YGMI helps with a number of web3 based job search problems.

Firstly it's crypto specific in it's job listings, this means all jobs posted relate to the blockchain space in some way.

We solve the problem of new projects needing to liquidate their own assets to use them for payment early on in development by accepting their native coins at their current pegged value. We offer massive discounts to new projects that have under a certain market cap enabling them to grow on the assets they have without turning to costly measures to recruit new talent.

We keep those assets under ownership until the project matures helping to support the project and it's growth in early stages. The typical job post on a competing site may run close to $299 USD to post a single job and it comes at the expense of the projects current cash flow.

If you're a more established company we allow you to pay in your native asset without first selling it to USDC/USDT or forcing fiat gateways. For those that don't have a native asset we allow for USDC/USDT payments via web3 wallets like Phantom and Metamask. A stripe based fiat option will launch in 2022, but crypto first is our mantra.

‍

We're going to pay people to apply


The reason we ultimately started building YGMI and the core feature we're working to bring you in the mid cycle of development is going to dramatically change the hiring culture and the incentive basis that draws users to the platform. We're going to pay them in your native coin if they're selected for an interview, or share part of your cost to post the job in our own native asset that can then be swapped for any crypto they would like internally on the platform itself.

We believe that you get more market participants when you enable them.

By giving leverage in equity back to those applying you enable a mindset that participation in the interview and hiring process isn't a loss even if it's not fruitful. This simple change to the hiring dynamic will be an absolute game changer as it incentivizes more effort on the applicants side by gamifying the process of the job search.

‍

The numbers and "Rep Scores"

‍

79% of people say that they use the social media for job searches in 2021, and youth up to 84%. Simply posting a job in one place doesn't cut it anymore, so we will be managing integrations into the most widely used platforms like Google experiences and meeting users on native platforms they're used to like Discord, Telegram, Twitter and Reddit. We will be building a host of bots that will auto post details about jobs that can be stored in both telegram and discord with daily and weekly feeds going to platforms like Twitter and Reddit from an internal team.

In 2017 a Funnel Benchmark report indicated that 1 in 6 applicants are asked to interview.

Companies today simply interview anyone who comes close to the standards their seeking, and this leads to applicant fatigue as people are considered for jobs that companies are almost 80% certain they won't end up hiring. An asset swap program will alleviate bad practices made by companies over interviewing and under hiring while giving a sense of leverage to candidates.

50% of applicants will say they will not work for a company with a bad reputation even for higher pay.

We will be building a feature that will allow users to articulate their interview and hiring experience, even beyond the initial phase. If an employer makes promises and doesn't keep it, you will be free to add an amended update to your comments on the business and only those selected to interact with a business will be allowed to leave reviews. This will create a scoring system for companies as they continue to use the platform and build reputation scores with their employees.

‍

Messaging and Scheduling


The ability to manage the recruiting process is a pain point for growing tech firms and we hope to declutter the tools needed to complete and end to end acquisition of talent. This means providing all necessary tools from the posting of a job, messaging of applicants, scheduling and video interview process, rep scores for businesses, incentive functions, and applicant tracking and sorting systems. In the future we will be aiming to build smart contract based agreements that can be sent by employers via web3 wallets and signed by minting an agreement token called "agreemint"

Lastly our Native Token YG3


YG3 will be the native token we use to support the platforms growth and development over the course of the next 3 years. We will be releasing this as a fair launch with no early seed rounds with unlocks, no early investors, just a simple fair launch with vision and community building from there on.

YG3 will be used as a native token for those companies that have no coin.

From investment firms looking for crypto analysts to defi blogs looking for content writers YG3 will be how we incentivize applicant pools to interview. Your payment in stables or fiat will be partially converted and buy back YG3 from the market at the current rate, and then be held in escrow under an assigned account until you select users for the interview process. YG3 has no planned DAO governance system in place and will be run internally. Part of the token supply will be held back to help support the growth of the project in development, as well as for promotional use during hiring campaigns and events.

PotionDao - web3 gaming DAO

PotionDao will be a DAO based web3 gaming organization.

Featuring:

  • Investing in web3 games on all chains that will be supporting the metaverse
  • Culture and Community building efforts to educate the less informed and grow as a group
  • Interesting partnerships that leverage the power of our community to market supported projects
  • Incubating early projects and tools related to DAO's and Gaming
  • Engaging gaming projects to find opportunities for builders in the DAO to earn pay and further PotionDao as a part of their game story
  • Development of metaverse design and model assets as rewards for members that can typically only be purchased in decentralized marketplaces.
  • Digital Land ownership owned by the collective and funded by the treasury with DAO token holding rewards that grant rights to use land spaces for events, advertising and other interesting features.

‍

PotionDao has a projected launch date sometime in mid to late 2022. We're currently working on much deeper aspects of the ideation of the core tools to be used, tokenomics and other important aspects to help the DAO blossom in early stages of it's launch.

We're still incredibly early on the growth and development of web3 gaming and the meterverse in general. For this reason we're taking the planning stages very serious and trying to imagine where this could go before letting anyone get involved investment wise.

We're a team of builders and dreams with a very specific goal of helping the smaller guy get a leg up in a safe way without allowing anyone to get left behind or lost on their journey.

Think of PotionDao as a group experiment where the very best of gaming culture and art are the staple ingredient in the financial growth of the group as a whole.

We hope to share more here as the ideation of this concept see's further development. We devote time to this weekly while building earlier products in the collective.
